Our Story

U Jarka is a beloved Polish restaurant nestled in the heart of Glasgow, bringing the authentic flavors of Poland to Scotland. Our name honors the tradition and warmth of Polish hospitality.

Every dish is prepared with recipes passed down through generations, using the finest ingredients to create an unforgettable dining experience. From our handmade pierogi to our rich beef stews, each plate tells a story of Polish culinary heritage.
